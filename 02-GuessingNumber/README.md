## Features

- ** Random Number Generation**: Each game generates a new random number
- ** Intelligent Hints**: Get "hot/cold" feedback based on your guesses
- ** Score Tracking**: Track your attempts and best scores
- ** Multiple Difficulty Levels**: Choose from Easy, Medium, or Hard
- ** Play Again Option**: Play multiple rounds in one session
- ** Persistent Best Score**: Track your best performance across sessions
- ** Clean Terminal Interface**: Colorful, organized output with visual separators
- ** Input Validation**: Handles invalid inputs gracefully
